## Animation System

### Keyframe Animations Added

**fadeIn** - Smooth opacity and subtle upward movement
- Used for: Hero titles, headers, text elements
- Duration: 0.6s ease-out

**slideInLeft** - Elements slide from left with fade
- Used for: Content on left side of hero sections
- Duration: 0.6s ease-out

**slideInRight** - Elements slide from right with fade
- Used for: Content on right side of hero sections
- Duration: 0.6s ease-out

**slideUp** - Elements slide up from bottom
- Used for: Card grids, project listings, featured paths
- Duration: 0.6s ease-out with staggered delays

**float** - Gentle floating motion
- Used for: Icon indicators, emoji accents
- Duration: 3s infinite ease-in-out

**glow** - Pulsing glow effect
- Used for: Call-to-action buttons, highlights
- Duration: 3s infinite ease-in-out
